# Sample Journeys (Examples)
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

These are examples to show what “progress” can look like.

## Biology Student → Health Analytics
Field → Role → Skills → Tools → Projects → Income

- Field: Biology
- Role: Health analytics junior / reporting
- Skills: data basics, SQL, visualization
- Tools: spreadsheets, a BI tool, a database
- Projects: health KPI dashboard + data cleanup pipeline
- Outcome: internship / entry role / freelance reporting work

## Teacher → Education Automation
- Field: Education
- Role: Ops/automation for a school or course business
- Skills: documentation, workflows, basic scripting/no-code
- Tools: forms, email automation, spreadsheets, AI assistant
- Projects: student onboarding automation + reporting
- Outcome: paid ops/automation gigs

## Sales/Business → CRM + Data Builder
- Field: Business
- Role: RevOps / analytics
- Skills: analytics, reporting, process design
- Tools: CRM, dashboards, automation tools
- Projects: CRM cleanup + pipeline dashboard
- Outcome: higher-leverage business roles
