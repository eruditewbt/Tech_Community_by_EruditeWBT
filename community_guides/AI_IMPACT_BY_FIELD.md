# AI Impact By Field
*Powered by EruditeWBT*

AI does not replace “jobs”.
It replaces **tasks**, and it changes what becomes valuable.

## How To Use This Page
1. Pick your field.
2. Identify tasks that will be automated or accelerated.
3. Choose the skills/tools that move you up the value chain.

## Common Shifts (Across Most Fields)
- Routine reporting → automated dashboards
- Basic drafting/writing → AI-assisted drafting + human review
- Manual data cleanup → automated pipelines + QA
- Simple support → AI triage + escalation + system design
